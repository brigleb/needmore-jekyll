Our friends at Salt, Fire & Time have long been a sustainable solution to the growing need for healthy, locally sourced ingredients in Portland.

Their newest venture, Broth Bar, is a natural extension of this ethos. This is a carefully curated space for eating well and in good company that opened doors today.
